## Project name and assets

The repository is distributed under the name **Loyalty Radar**. You may accurately describe an unmodified distribution as Loyalty Radar and may state that a fork is based on Loyalty Radar.

Do not use the Loyalty Radar name, icon, screenshots, or visual identity in a way that:

- implies endorsement by the project maintainer;
- misrepresents a modified fork as the official release;
- suggests affiliation with a loyalty program or source;
- obscures material changes, added data collection, or different privacy behavior.

Forks should use a distinguishable product name when they change network behavior, collect credentials, add hosted services, or materially alter report conclusions.

## Third-party screenshots and logos

Public demo assets should not include third-party logos unless their use has been reviewed for the intended context. Prefer plain-text source and program names. Do not copy publisher artwork, loyalty-card designs, article screenshots, or forum avatars into the repository without permission or a documented legal basis.

Questions about this policy may be opened as a repository issue, provided they contain no confidential or personal information.
